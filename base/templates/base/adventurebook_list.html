Adventure Books

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Harry discovers he's a wizard on his 11th birthday when Hagrid escorts him to magic-teaching Hogwarts School. As a baby, his mother's love protected him and vanquished the dark lord, leaving Harry with a lightning-bolt scar.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Harry returns to Hogwarts for his second year, which proves to be more challenging than the first. The Chamber of Secrets has been opened, leaving students petrified by an unleashed monster.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

In his third year at Hogwarts, Harry learns that the notorious prisoner Sirius Black has escaped from Azkaban prison and is coming for him. The truth about Sirius, and Harry's own past, is revealed.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

During Harry's fourth year at Hogwarts, he is unwillingly entered into the Triwizard Tournament. Facing daunting tasks and dark forces, Harry must navigate the most dangerous year yet.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Harry's fifth year brings new challenges, including the rise of the dark forces led by Voldemort and the new authoritarian regime at Hogwarts. Harry forms 'Dumbledore's Army' to fight back.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

In his sixth year, Harry discovers a mysterious potions book belonging to the 'Half-Blood Prince.' As he delves deeper into Voldemort's past, Harry prepares for the ultimate battle.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part-1

In the final book, Harry, Ron, and Hermione leave Hogwarts to find and destroy Voldemort's Horcruxes. The wizarding world is at war, and Harry's journey ends with a showdown against Voldemort.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part-2
