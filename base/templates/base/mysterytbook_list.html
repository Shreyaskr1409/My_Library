Mystery Books

The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo

This gripping novel follows journalist Mikael Blomkvist as he teams up with the enigmatic hacker Lisbeth Salander to uncover the truth behind a decades-old disappearance. The story delves into dark family secrets and corruption, keeping readers on the edge of their seats.

Gone Girl

A psychological thriller about the disappearance of Amy Dunne, which quickly spirals into a twisted and unsettling narrative. Her husband Nick becomes the prime suspect, and as the investigation unfolds, shocking truths about their marriage come to light.

Big Little Lies

A compelling tale of three women in a tight-knit community whose seemingly perfect lives unravel to reveal hidden secrets and shocking lies. The story culminates in a mysterious death that ties their fates together.

The Da Vinci Code

Harvard symbologist Robert Langdon and cryptologist Sophie Neveu embark on a thrilling quest across Europe to solve a murder mystery linked to secrets within Leonardo da Vinci's art. Their journey exposes a hidden religious history and a powerful conspiracy.

The Hound of the Baskervilles

One of Sherlock Holmes' most famous cases, where he and Dr. Watson investigate the legend of a monstrous hound terrorizing the Baskerville family. Set on the eerie moors of Devon, this classic novel combines elements of the supernatural with Holmes' sharp detective work.

In the Woods

This debut novel from the Dublin Murder Squad series follows detective Rob Ryan, who is haunted by a childhood trauma linked to a current murder case. The investigation reveals deeply buried secrets, and Ryan must confront his past to solve the crime.

The Silent Patient

Alicia Berenson, a celebrated painter, shoots her husband and then stops speaking entirely. Forensic psychologist Theo Faber is determined to unravel the mystery behind her silence, leading to a suspenseful and shocking revelation.

The No. 1 Ladies' Detective Agency

Follow the adventures of Precious Ramotswe, Botswana's first female detective, as she solves a variety of cases with wit and wisdom. This charming series blends mystery with rich cultural insights and endearing characters.

The Cuckoo's Calling

Private investigator Cormoran Strike investigates the death of supermodel Lula Landry, initially ruled a suicide. As Strike delves deeper, he uncovers a tangled web of secrets and lies within the fashion industry and Lula's personal life.

Rebecca

This gothic mystery tells the story of a young woman who marries the wealthy widower Maxim de Winter and moves to his estate, Manderley. She soon finds herself overshadowed by the lingering presence of his first wife, Rebecca, whose memory haunts the mansion and its inhabitants.

The Woman in White

In the thrilling conclusion to J.K. Rowling's beloved Harry Potter series. The story picks up immediately after the events of Part 1, following Harry, Ron, and Hermione as they continue their quest to destroy the remaining Horcruxes and defeat Lord Voldemort.

The Secret History

A modern classic, this novel follows a group of eccentric students at an elite college who are drawn into a murder plot. The story, narrated by one of the students, explores themes of guilt, obsession, and moral ambiguity.
